About

Welcome to Gregory’s Optical. Since 1989, Gregory’s Optical has offered eyewear to discerning customers who prefer the highest level of quality and service, along with a selection of frames unrivaled in Rhode Island and beyond. Coupling the artisanal legacy of a neighborhood optical with a design-forward selection of a cosmopolitan boutique, we are committed to beauty, excellence and integrity.
Our exclusive collections are manufactured in Europe, Japan and the United States, and we decline to sell any brands that dominate the market, but do not live up to our production standards. From timeless iconic classics to one-of-a-kind eyewear, we curate a selection of frames that will delight everyone, from the passionate eyewear enthusiast to the tentative traditionalist. Despite our principles and selection, our glasses are competitively priced and address a full range of budgets and needs.

Vibe

Gregory Sirota opened the shop post-Soviet style: direct, no frills, scrupulous to a flaw. In partnership with his son Michael, they established a reputation of commitment to craft, beauty and durability unmatched in the industry. Today his son Michael carries on the legacy of Gregory’s vision, delivering exceptionally designed, perfectly crafted glasses to people who care.

Services and Craft

At Gregory’s Optical laboratory work is completed by hand on the premises. From rimless to progressive lenses, we fill all of prescriptions with the highest quality, most technologically advanced lenses. We take satisfaction in and assume responsibility for every pair of glasses that we craft.
